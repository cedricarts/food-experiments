Balanced Meal (Experimental Recipe)

A balanced meal recipe that combines protein, vegetables, and grains for a nutritious and satisfying dish.

Ingredients (1 serving, depending on portions)
  • Rice:2 cups
  • Salt: ½ teaspoon
  • Eggs: 2 Large
  • Avocado: 1/4 Large avocado
  • Vegetable oil or butter: (For eggs)
Steps
  1. Cook rice according to package instructions.
  2. Scramble eggs in a pan with a little oil or butter.
  3. Prepare avocado slices.
  4. Assemble the meal by combining the cooked rice, scrambled eggs, and avocado slices.

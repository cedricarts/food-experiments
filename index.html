Tortilla Making (Experimental Recipe)

A simple flour tortilla you can make while thinking about your next game idea.

Ingredients
  • Flour: 250g (about 2 cups)
  • Cooking Oil: 2 teaspoons
  • Warm Water: 180ml
  • Salt: A pinch
Steps
  1. Combine the flour and pinch of salt in a bowl.
  2. Add the 2 tsp of cooking oil and stir into the flour.
  3. Gradually pour in the 180ml warm water while mixing.
  4. Knead until you have a smooth, soft dough (about 3–5 minutes).
  5. Divide the dough into small balls (6–8, depending on size).
  6. Flatten each ball with a rolling pin to about 1–2 mm thickness.
  7. Heat a dry pan over medium heat, and cook each tortilla ~1 min per side.
  8. Tortillas should puff slightly and show light brown spots.
  9. Serve warm. Enjoy!
